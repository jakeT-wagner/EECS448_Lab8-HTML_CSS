Uncle Iroh



"Hope is something you give yourself. That is the meaning of inner strength"
Uncle Iroh, beloved character in the hit television show Avatar the Last Airbender, wowed fans multifold with his wisdom, comedic relief, and firebending prowess. Iroh was a lot of things to many people in the Avatar universe, notably as a general for the Fire Nation, member of the White Lotus, and friend to all. Iroh was also an uncle to Zuko, molding him into his own man. He also befriended the Gaang, and provided them wisom many times over the course of the show. During the end of the 100 Year War, he, alongside other White Lotus members, re-captured Ba Sing Se from the Fire Nation, helping to bring balance back to the World. Upon his death, Iroh joined the Spirit World, a place not many humans venture. They say that you can still meet Iroh there, where he dishes out wisdom to all.
